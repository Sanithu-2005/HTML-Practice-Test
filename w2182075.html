About the Event

The Employability Support Fair is part of Engagement Week and is designed to help students prepare for placements, internships, and graduate careers.

This year’s event includes:

  • CV and cover letter workshops
  • LinkedIn profile reviews
  • Mock interviews with industry professionals
  • Networking opportunities with employers

Date: Wednesday 12th March, 2025

Time: 11:00 AM – 3:00 PM

Location: Cavendish Campus Atrium

Here is the schedule of sessions taking place during the day:

Department / Service Session Time
Careers Team CV Workshop 11:00 AM
Interview Skills Session 12:00 PM
Industry Panel Employer Q&A 1:30 PM
Alumni Office Graduate Success Stories 2:30 PM
All sessions are free for registered students. Please bring your student ID.
